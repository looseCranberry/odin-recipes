Ravager Dog

Restores 4320 health over 30 seconds, however you must remain seated while eating and keep your elbows off the table. If you spend at least 10 second shoveling down your food, you will become well fed and gain power and spirit.

(Don't ask what it's made of)

Ingredients:
  • Ravager Flesh
  • Salt + Pepper
  • Eye of Newt
  • Tongue of African Swallow
Steps:
  1. Slow roast the ravager flesh overnight
  2. Boil 4 quarts of water, mix in flesh and eye of newt
  3. Garnish with tongue of Africa swallow
Enjoy!
